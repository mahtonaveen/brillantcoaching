 Intensive Boards Preparation

We arrange regular one / two / three year(s) course for the students preparing to appear for Board examinations. We provide complete guidance for the science group subjects (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics and Biology) for ICSE / CBSE - X Board (Foundation Course) and ISC / CBSE - XII Board examination as well. Our teachers are well-versed with the syllabus and question patterns that every Board covers. We help the students cover the syllabus in way in advance so that they can devote ample time to solve test papers that are personally prepared and handed out to our students by our faculty members. We hold separate doubt clearing sessions after the completion of each chapter so that every student gets the opportunity to go forward with a clear concept.

We also organize regular weekly tests to assess their ability week by week, assess the subjects they have an affinity for and the subjects they need to work on harder, so that our faculty can pay special attention on their weaknesses and systematically work on them.
